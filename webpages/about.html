About Me

I’m Jermaine, an IT Technician with over 6 years of hands-on experience,
now on an exciting journey to become a Full-Stack Web Developer. Originally
from the island of Dominica, I migrated to the USA with a deep curiosity for
technology that started early—at just 10 years old,learning to type on a typewriter.
That spark grew into a lifelong passion for problem-solving, taking things apart,
putting them back together, and unknowingly absorbing the foundations of IT long
before I earned my certifications.

In my IT career, I’ve mastered troubleshooting, system optimization, and client
support, but web development offers something new—an opportunity to build, create,
and bring ideas to life on the digital canvas. For me, coding is more than a career
path: it’s fun, challenging, and endlessly rewarding. I thrive on learning and pushing
boundaries, and I’m committed to crafting powerful, user-friendly web experiences.

This portfolio is not just a showcase of my work, but a reflection of my journey—from
a curious kid in Dominica to an IT professional in the USA, now embracing the future as a
Full-Stack Web Developer.
